This map shows Housing Counseling Agencies which will help with preventative foreclosure counseling.

Only two do not help.

This is also positive. Foreclosure and home loss is one of the most devestating experiences for any person or family

This map shows Housing Counseling Agencies which will help with seniors explicity.

Only three are tailored to serve seniors explicitly

There should probably be more centers with senior support because housing becomes a greater burden as one ages.

This map shows Housing Counseling Agencies which participate in pre-purchase counseling

Pre-purchase counseling is a "program [which] covers topics like financial management, evaluating credit information, prequalifying & loan approval, financing options, shopping for a home, finalizing the loan, the closing process, maintaining your new home, avoiding foreclosure and predatory lending practices.".

HUD sponsors housing counseling agencies which provide this service.

All but two agencies in Philadelphia provide this service.
